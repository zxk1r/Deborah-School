Our Story

Shaping the Leaders
of Tomorrow

Deborah School is a prestigious educational institution dedicated to nurturing academic excellence, discipline, and leadership among students. Our mission is to provide a strong foundation that empowers learners to succeed in an ever-changing world.

Through innovative teaching methods, experienced educators, and a supportive learning environment, we ensure that every student reaches their full potential — not only in knowledge, but in character, responsibility, and vision.

Director's
Message

"Education is not merely the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire. At Deborah School, we light that fire in every child."

Welcome to Deborah School. We are committed to shaping future leaders through discipline, hard work, and unwavering excellence. Our goal is to inspire students to achieve greatness while maintaining strong values and integrity.

Every child who walks through our doors carries unlimited potential. Our role is to create the environment where that potential flourishes — academically, socially, and morally. Together, we build a brighter future for Ethiopia and the world.

Balebet Deborah Director, Deborah School
